Our Story

Welcome to Nihon, Arigato – A Japanese Café. Our story beings in 1986, Okinawa, Japan. Sakura and Hanzo Hasashi began their journey by cooking for their neighbors in the basement of their home. The community would visit every night just to try their Japanese style cooking. Soon after, lines started flowing from their basement to the front of their home. As news travels fast, Sakura and Hanzo Hasashi gained popularity throughout Japan and caught the attention of the European and American culinary world. Today, Nihon, Arigato stays true to its roots, opening doors in San Francisco, Ca. and Williamsburg, New York. Receiving 2 Michelin Stars and still serving in basement-style settings, Sakura and Hanzo Hasashi remain in their home where they started their journey. To this day, they are still serving the community with their mom-and-pop style cuisine...
